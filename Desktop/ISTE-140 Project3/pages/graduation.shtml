GRADUATION

Do you have a homeschooled student graduating this year? The annual formal graduation will be held on June 10th, 2017 at Holden Chapel in Holden, Massachusetts. This cap and gown event gives Massachusetts homeschool families the opportunity to honor the achievments of their students. Graduates receive a diploma and a rose to give to their mother. The ceremony is followed by a cake reception. Please contact Susan Jacobsen at graduation@masshope.org for more information.
