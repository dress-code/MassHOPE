USED CURRICULUM SALE

Are you shopping for curriculum for next school year, or do you have unused books hanging around your house? Get your curriculum for a lower price or make some extra money by checking out our annual used curriculum sale on June 17th, 2017 at Chapel of the Cross in Westborough, MA! Each year, MassHOPE's UCS facilitates the exchange of thousands of books ranging from science curriculum to Nancy Drew. There are books for everyone in your family! To register as a seller, please contact info@masshope.org for more information.
